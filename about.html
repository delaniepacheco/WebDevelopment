About Me

My full name is Delanie Pacheco Quintana. I am a first generation Mexican American college student. I have not always known what I wanted to do as a career. I only knew that I had to make my parents proud. As the first daughter to my Mexican parents I have always felt the need to be successful so that I could help them out. My parents did so much for me and provided me with everything I have ever needed. They did this with struggles of language barriers, missing their family, and culture differences. Since I started school I always tried by best because I loved making my parents proud. It was hard because I would feel like it was never enough. I always wanted to be better and better. I grew up feeling pretty misunderstood by a lot of people around me. I truly did not see my future when I was younger. As I started to get older and seeing life in a different way I have been able to realize that I truly love to learn. I love to learn new things and experience the world around me. I love to travel and I love to meet new people and build meaningful connections. I am a very deep thinker, which is maybe why I always felt misunderstood. I felt like I would think of things others wouldn't. However, I realize thats not the case. I realized I had to be more open and let people actually get to know me. I found my love for learning about technology when I started college back in 2020. It was something that was challenging for me. I wanted to know I could learn to do something new from start to finish. I then started to see how much I love to be a leader. I would like to have a leadership/management career in an IT field in the future. I feel like I am a good team player and good at communicating to lead a team to success. At this moment my goal is to get my bachelors degree and find myself a career where I can thrive and interact with people.

I lost my dad August 31st of this year... Not too long ago. I hope to my Dad proud.. from where ever he may be. I love you Dad.
